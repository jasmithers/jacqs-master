Special Events

Contact me for a quote by hitting the request a quote button above! I have photo booth packages and I can create a custom package for your event! I will photograph anniversaries, birthdays, corporate events, etc. Event packages normally start around $500; the photo booth package would be $200 includes: backdrop, lighting, and some props if needed. I will also have an assistant to help me along the way with capturing the special moments and fun times at your event. Weddings To be honest, I have never photographed a wedding before however I know one day soon I will get to photograph a Halifax/HRM wedding and watch the happy couple smile as they look at the outcome of their photographs! My goal is to capture the memories and moments of the big day! I will try my best to get all the moments on your wish list! I have been trained by Nova Scotia Community College on how to photograph a wedding. I have the gear and the creativeness to get started! Wedding packages normally start at $1,500; please hit the request a quote button and you will get a custom wedding package that even includes Save the Date Designs! I suggest booking an engagement shoot with me; if you are happy with the outcome and wish to book me for your wedding you will have the cost of the engagement shoot included in the wedding quote.
