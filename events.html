Special Events

I have photo booth packages and I can create a custom package for your event, whether it’s anniversaries, birthdays, corporate events and beyond. Event packages normally start around $500. Photo booth packages start at $200 and include: backdrop, lighting, and some props if needed. I will also have an assistant to help me along the way with capturing the special moments and fun times at your event.

Weddings

I love to watch the smiles of the happy couple as I capture them and on the big day, and when they see the outcome of their photographs! Together we will make a wish list of important moments to capture on your big day, so you can focus on creating the memories that I’ll frame for you forever.

Wedding packages start at $1,500, request a quote to get more information for packages that include Save the Date designs. I always recommend booking an engagement shoot with me first.
